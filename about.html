About Us

26 Years of Excellence in Skill Development

Our Story

Training Center Nepal (TCN) was established in 1998 with a vision to empower Nepali youth through quality skill development programs. Over the past 26 years, we have grown to become one of Nepal's most trusted training institutions.

We have successfully trained over 40,000 students across 45+ districts, partnering with international organizations like JICA, ADB, and GIZ to deliver high-impact training programs that create sustainable livelihoods.

Our commitment to quality education and practical skill development has made us a preferred partner for government agencies, NGOs, and corporate organizations throughout Nepal.

Our Mission

To empower individuals and communities through quality, accessible, and industry-relevant skill development programs that bridge the gap between education and employment.

Our Vision

To be Nepal's leading skill development center, recognized for excellence in training and our contribution to national development through empowered youth.
